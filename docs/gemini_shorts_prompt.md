# 제미나이 AI 쇼츠 프롬프트
# 소리볼륨 조절기 — "노트북 볼륨 분노" 편

> **사용 도구**: Google Gemini (Veo 2) / VideoFX
> **포맷**: 9:16 세로, 30~45초, 한국 대학생 배경

---

## 장면별 프롬프트 (씬 분할 입력)

---

### 🎬 씬 1 — 평온한 시작 (0~4초)

**프롬프트:**
```
A Korean college student in their early 20s sits at a desk with a laptop open,
wearing casual clothes (hoodie). The room is cozy — textbooks and snacks visible.
They look relaxed, about to adjust the volume on their laptop.
Warm indoor lighting. Cinematic 9:16 vertical shot. Realistic style.
```

**자막**: `노트북으로 볼륨 좀 올리려고 했는데...`

---

### 🎬 씬 2 — 이상함을 눈치채기 시작 (4~12초)

**프롬프트:**
```
Close-up of a laptop screen showing a volume slider UI.
The person's hand moves the slider UP — but the volume indicator drops DOWN to a lower level.
The person tilts their head confused, squinting at the screen.
They try again — same result. Slight frown forming.
Soft natural lighting, 9:16 vertical, realistic close-up shot.
```

**자막**: `어? 왜 소리가 더 작아지지?`

---

### 🎬 씬 3 — 점점 당황 + 약오름 (12~22초)

**프롬프트:**
```
A Korean college student staring intensely at their laptop, repeatedly moving
the volume slider up and down with increasing frustration.
Each time they move it up, the volume bar goes DOWN.
Their expression shifts from confused → annoyed → visibly angry.
They lean closer to the screen, jabbing at the trackpad aggressively.
One hand runs through their hair in frustration.
Cinematic 9:16 vertical. Close alternating shots: face reaction + screen close-up.
Realistic style, expressive acting.
```

**자막**: `올리면 내려가고... 내리면 올라가고...`

---

### 🎬 씬 4 — 슬라이더 도망 + 최고조 분노 (22~32초)

**프롬프트:**
```
Extreme close-up of a laptop trackpad — the person's finger chases a volume slider
thumb that keeps sliding away every time they get close to it.
Cut to: the student's face in full rage mode — eyes wide, jaw clenched,
dramatically slamming both hands on the desk (but not breaking anything).
They shout silently at the screen (mouth open in disbelief).
Over-the-top comedic frustration expression.
9:16 vertical, fast-cut editing energy, dramatic lighting on face.
```

**자막**: `슬라이더가... 도망가고 있어??`

---

### 🎬 씬 5 — 반전 + CTA (32~42초)

**프롬프트:**
```
The student suddenly stops. Looks directly at camera with a sly, mischievous grin
(like they just realized something). They copy a link on their laptop and
open a KakaoTalk chat, pasting the link to send to a friend.
Smug, satisfied expression — "now it's someone else's problem."
Pull back to show the full setup. Warm lighting. 9:16 vertical.
```

**자막**: `ㅋㅋ 친구한테 보내야지 😈`

---

## 통합 단일 프롬프트 (한 번에 입력할 경우)

```
Create a 40-second vertical (9:16) short video. Realistic cinematic style.
Korean college student setting (cozy dorm room, laptop, casual hoodie).

Scene 1 (0-4s): Relaxed student opens laptop to adjust volume. Calm mood.

Scene 2 (4-12s): Close-up — student moves volume slider UP but volume goes DOWN.
Confused head tilt, tries again. Same result.

Scene 3 (12-22s): Growing frustration — repeated attempts fail.
Expression: confused → irritated → angry. Aggressively tapping trackpad.

Scene 4 (22-32s): Slider runs AWAY from cursor on screen.
Student slams desk in comedic rage. Silent scream at screen. Over-the-top reaction.

Scene 5 (32-42s): Student pauses, gets sly grin, copies link,
sends it to a KakaoTalk friend. Smug face to camera. Fade out.

Style: Expressive comedic acting, fast cuts between face and screen close-ups,
dramatic lighting on frustration scenes, warm cozy room ambiance.
Mood: funny, relatable, meme-worthy. No dialogue, only expressions and text overlays.
```

---

## 자막 타임라인 (편집용)

| 시간 | 자막 | 폰트 스타일 |
|------|------|------------|
| 0~4s | 노트북으로 볼륨 좀 올리려고 했는데... | 흰색, 하단 중앙 |
| 4~12s | 어? 왜 소리가 더 작아지지? 🤔 | 흰색 + 노란 이모지 |
| 12~22s | 올리면 내려가고... 내리면 올라가고... | 빨간색 강조 |
| 22~32s | 슬라이더가... 도망가고 있어?? 😱 | 빨간색, 떨리는 효과 |
| 32~42s | ㅋㅋ 친구한테 보내야지 😈 | 노란색, 굵게 |
| 38~42s | [서비스 링크 + QR] | 하단 고정 |

---

## BGM 추천

- **장르**: 유쾌한 코미디 / 개그 효과음
- **YouTube Audio Library 검색어**: `funny frustration`, `comedic tension`, `ukulele quirky`
- **효과음 포인트**:
  - 씬 2: "삐" 오류음
  - 씬 4: 슬라이더 도망 시 "whoosh" 효과음
  - 씬 5: 반전 "ding" 또는 악당 웃음소리

---

## 제미나이 입력 팁

1. **VideoFX 접속** → `Create video` 클릭
2. 씬별로 나눠서 입력하거나 통합 프롬프트 한 번에 입력
3. **Style 옵션**: `Cinematic` 또는 `Realistic` 선택
4. **Aspect ratio**: `9:16` (Shorts/Reels 세로형) 필수 선택
5. 생성 후 CapCut 또는 클립챔프로 자막·BGM 추가
